About Us

At Cbeez Toonie we leverage the use of availabe technologies (nanny cams,online payment and geo locators) to provide safe and convinient childcare services to parents from the comfort of their child's home. All you need is to download the app or visit our website to be able to view,book, rate and review our toonies(care givers). Our toonies are trained and equiped with a nany cam,from the app, a client/parent scans toonie's badge(which is linked to the camera) and connects the app with live camera feeds so they can remotely monitor the childcare session. The same feed is monitored by our emergency response team at our offices. Once care session is done, the client/ parent scans out toonie's badge,makes payment and leaves a rating/ review of the toonie. We also have a cab hailing module where our toonies can request our fleet of cabs for pickup and/or drop off for their safety.
